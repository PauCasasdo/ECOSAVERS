SUSTAINABLE DEVELOPMENT GOALS

Our SDG Commitments

Each activity, workshop and campus we organize is designed with the goal of generating a positive impact not only on animals and nature, but also on people and the construction of a more just, equitable and sustainable society.

From ECOSAVERS we believe that the protection of the environment and respect for living beings are the foundation for a more just and sustainable future for all people.

SDG 15: Life of Terrestrial Ecosystems

CONSERVATION · BIODIVERSITY · PROTECTION

Why is it our main SDG?

Terrestrial life is the foundation of all ECOSAVERS activities. Through our conservation, observation and education activities on fauna and flora, we contribute directly to the protection of terrestrial ecosystems and biodiversity.

How do we achieve it?

Through the activity "Habitat Guardians", participants of all ages learn to observe, identify and protect local species. Solidarity routes and volunteer days at Habitat contribute directly to the restoration and maintenance of natural ecosystems.

Our impact

Improving natural habitats, restoring green spaces and fostering awareness about the importance of each species within the ecosystem. Each of our actions contributes to slowing biodiversity loss and protecting terrestrial life.

SDG 4: Quality Education

LEARNING · TRAINING · KNOWLEDGE

Experiential education for everyone

Environmental education is the heart of our mission. We provide inclusive, quality and equitable education through activities that connect theory with practical experience.

How do we achieve it?

With specialized workshops, nature camps, guided visits to shelters and volunteer days. Our entire team is dedicated to creating educational experiences that mark the lives of participants and inspire them to be defenders of the planet.

Our impact

Children, young people and adults acquire skills in biodiversity, sustainability and environmental responsibility. They develop critical thinking and problem-solving abilities related to nature conservation.

SDG 3: Health and Wellbeing

PHYSICAL WELLBEING · MENTAL WELLBEING · QUALITY OF LIFE

Connection with nature as a source of health

Numerous studies show that contact with animals and nature improves mental health, reduces stress and promotes overall wellbeing. All our activities promote this healthy connection.

How do we achieve it?

We create spaces of connection with animals and nature that improve emotional balance. Volunteering at "Helping Hands" fosters a sense of community and purpose. Educational activities promote physical activity and sports in contact with nature.

Our impact

Improvement of emotional and mental wellbeing of participants. Creation of more supportive and compassionate communities. Promotion of healthy and sustainable lifestyle habits.

SDG 12: Responsible Production and Consumption

CONSCIOUS CONSUMPTION · RESPONSIBILITY · SUSTAINABILITY

We promote conscious choices regarding animals

Through the activity "Adopt with Knowledge", we motivate critical reflection on consumption choices related to animals, promoting responsible adoption instead of thoughtless purchasing.

How do we achieve it?

Education about the consequences of buying animals without responsibility. Promotion of adoption from shelters. Awareness about animal mistreatment related to consumption. Promotion of respectful practices with animals.

Our impact

Reduction in demand for animals raised in poor conditions. More animals saved through adoption. Change of mentality in society regarding animal treatment.

SDG 13: Climate Action

CLIMATE CHANGE · ENVIRONMENTAL EDUCATION · LOCAL ACTION

Biodiversity as a shield against climate change

The protection of terrestrial ecosystems is fundamental to combat climate change. Forests, green areas and local fauna are crucial for absorbing carbon and maintaining climate regulation on the planet.

How do we achieve it?

Education about the connection between biodiversity and climate. Habitat restoration actions that help capture carbon. Promotion of sustainable lifestyles. Participation in reforestation and environmental recovery projects.

Our impact

Creating awareness about climate urgency. Concrete environmental restoration actions. Empowering participants to act against climate change through environmental education.

SDG 17: Partnerships to Achieve the Goals

COLLABORATION · COMMUNITY · NETWORK OF CHANGE

Together we are stronger

No SDG can be achieved alone. ECOSAVERS works in partnership with schools, institutions, volunteers and the local community to multiply our impact.

How do we achieve it?

Collaboration with educational centers for integrated projects. Partnerships with shelters and environmental organizations. Active involvement of volunteers in all our actions. Creation of community networks for change.

Our impact

Multiplication of impact through partnerships. Building a strong and committed community. Development of more ambitious and transformative projects. Creating a sustainable network for change.

"The Sustainable Development Goals are not just numbers — they are promises to make the planet a better place for future generations. At ECOSAVERS, we work every day to make these promises a reality."
